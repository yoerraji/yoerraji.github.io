Our Startup


We are a team of two passionate young entrepreneurs from 42 Network (Students in 1337 School), who decided to build a Multiplayer Mobile Fantasy & Strategy Game named Mr.Gangnama. The game is more than just a gaming app, it is made for players to learn as they play. It is a post-apocalyptic landscape (about 10 to 15 years after civilization collapsed). The player must learn survival strategies in order to stay alive, he must fight others as well for ground, oil, gold, and many recourses. There are tanks, aircraft, and infantry that the player can fight with.

Yousser Erraji, Founder of Mr.Gangnama | yoerraji@student.1337.ma
